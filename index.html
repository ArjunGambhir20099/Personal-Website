About me

I build for impact, at the intersection of Neuroscience, AI & Robotics,

Seventeen years old, driven by the belief that most exciting discoveries happen when different fields collide. My world sits where neuroscience meets AI, where robots borrow ideas from nature, and where curiosity turns into impact.

Youngest in the exclusive rooms, I am chasing a single question – What is Intelligence? That question has taken me from studying the human brain to exploring AI safety, building swarm robots, creating opportunities for hundreds of students, and learning how technology can solve real problems.

A little too curious. This isn't a portfolio. It's the story of an ongoing experiment—following curiosity wherever it leads.
