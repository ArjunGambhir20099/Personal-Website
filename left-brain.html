Students are using AI every day, but most are never taught how these tools work, what risks they carry, or when to question them. SEEK AI simplifies the EU AI Act 2024 and UNESCO AI Guidelines into an interactive AI literacy platform for high school students, helping them explore bias, privacy, safety, and responsible AI use through practical tools and bite-sized lessons.

Mobile App Machine Learning Healthtech App International Award Winner

Pocket Doc.

Pocket Doc is an AI-powered mobile app that uses image classification to screen for possible signs of nutritional deficiencies. Built using images from NHS and other validated sources, it was designed as an early health-awareness tool, not a replacement for medical diagnosis. The app received Judge's Favourite in the mobile category at Coolest Projects Global 2024, which welcomed 7,197 participants and 4,678 projects from 43 countries.

2020 First App Offline Learning STEM for Good

LEARN

LEARN was the first app I ever built, at the age of 10, during the 2020 lockdown. It helped children learn English at home without needing internet access, at a time when many students were struggling to attend online classes. More than a technical project, LEARN became my first real step into STEM for good—showing me that even a 10-year-old could build something that helped others. I never looked back after this, and it still holds a special place in my heart.
